## Detailed Analysis Instructions per Figure

### D1: NPC vs Shuttle Size (`D1_npc_vs_shuttle.png`)
**Section:** 4.1 Optimization Results
**What to analyze:**
1. Identify the U-shaped (or convex) cost curve for each case
2. Locate the minimum NPC point for each case and report exact values
3. Compare the slope of ascent vs descent -- what drives each side?
4. Explain WHY the optimal shuttle size differs across cases (Case 1: 1,000 m3 vs Case 2: 5,000 m3 vs Case 3: 5,000 m3)
5. Quantify the cost penalty of suboptimal sizing (e.g., "choosing 5,000 m3 for Case 1 increases NPC by X%")

**Required numbers from CSV:** `NPC_Total_USDm` for optimal and +/- 1 size from each case.

---

5. Calculate compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of total cost

**Required numbers:** `Total_Year_Cost_USDm` for years 2030, 2040, 2050 from per-year files.
