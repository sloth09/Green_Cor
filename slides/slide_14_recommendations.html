Strategic Recommendations

1Prioritize Ulsan Route Development

Deploy infrastructure investment toward Case 2-2 (Ulsan → Busan) route to achieve 38% cost savings and lowest NPC ($94.9M)

2Optimize Fleet Composition

Use 5,000 m³ shuttle vessels for Cases 2-1 and 2-2. For Case 1, consider smaller 2,500 m³ vessels if storage infrastructure is committed

3Reassess Busan Storage Investment

High tank CAPEX ($28.9M) in Case 1 limits cost competitiveness. Only viable if geographic positioning or other factors require local storage

4Phased Implementation

Deploy 6-8 shuttles initially (2030), with 10% annual growth through 2050 to meet demand forecasts and minimize stranded assets
