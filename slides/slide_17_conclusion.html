Conclusion

Primary Finding

The Ulsan route (Case 2-2) is the economically optimal solution, achieving a Net Present Cost of $94.9M over 20 years—a 38.2% cost reduction compared to the Busan storage-based scenario.

Strategic Implications

Development priorities should focus on establishing bunkering infrastructure in Ulsan with optimal 5,000 m³ shuttle fleet and 2,000 m³/h pumping capacity to serve Green Corridor vessels economically and sustainably through the 2050 horizon.

Next Steps

Proceed with detailed feasibility analysis for Ulsan infrastructure, including port authority coordination, equipment procurement timelines, and phased fleet deployment through 2050.
